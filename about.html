About – SDG 12 & Food Loss

SDG 12 focuses on ensuring sustainable consumption and production patterns. Food loss is a major environmental, social, and economic concern. Around 1.3 billion tons of food is lost globally every year, even though millions of people face hunger.

This website aims to:

  • Educate people about food loss and its causes
  • Promote responsible consumption habits
  • Provide practical solutions to reduce food waste
  • Encourage upcycling and sustainable lifestyle practices
Why Focus on Food Loss?

✔ Food loss wastes natural resources like water, land, and energy.
✔ It produces methane – a harmful greenhouse gas.
✔ Reducing food loss improves food security.
✔ It supports environmental sustainability.

Creating awareness to reduce food loss and promote sustainable living.
