Reducing Food Loss & Promoting Sustainable Consumption (SDG 12)

SDG 12 – Why It Matters

SDG 12 promotes responsible consumption and production. One major issue under this goal is food loss. One-third of all food produced globally is lost before it is eaten. This impacts the environment, economy, and global hunger levels. Our website spreads awareness and provides solutions to reduce food loss at home, industries, and communities.
